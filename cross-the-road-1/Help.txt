-------- HELP FOR PLAYER --------
       Press 'w' to go up. 
       Press 's' to go up. 
       Press 'a' to go left. 
       Press 'd' to go right. 
	     
               ------

     Press 's' to save the game.
     Press 'p' to stop the game.
	
               ------
      After finishing the game:  
Press 'r' to return to the menu screen.
     Press 'e' to exit the game.
