-------- HELP FOR PLAYER --------
        Press W to go up. 
        Press S to go up. 
       Press A to go left. 
       Press W to go right. 
	     
              ------

     Press W to save the game.
     Press P to stop the game.
	
              ------
     After finishing the game:  
Press ESC to return to the menu screen.
     Press E to exit the game.
